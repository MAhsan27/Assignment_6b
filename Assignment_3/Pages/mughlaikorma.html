Mughlai Korma

Mughlai Chicken Korma is chicken korma made with exotic spices in a tangy yogurt sauce served over rice. This dish has been praised for centuries by the Mughal Royal Kitchen and Indian Cuisine as one of the world's most popular dishes. The flavors of Mughlai cuisine range from mild to spicy, with a unique aromatic quality.
