Ras Malai

Ras malai, originally [citation needed] known as rosomalai, or roshmalai, is a dessert from the Indian subcontinent. The dessert is called roshmalai/rosmalai in Bengali,[1] ras malai in Hindi, and rasa malei in Odia. It is popular in India, Bangladesh and Pakistan. The origin of the sweet is impossible to verify but there are alternate stories identifying an origin in the Bengal region.
